WHO IS THIS? THE DEBUT ALBUM KNOW MORE
One again, Phanum brings you the last news from the Culture World, this month with a 11 years old piano Master: Joey Alexander. He has played piano since the age of six, and found an affinity to jazz early on after being introduced to the genre though his father’s record collection. Originally from Jakarta, Indonesia, Alexander has already performed all over the world. However, he made his United States début at the Jazz at Lincoln Center annual fundraiser earlier this year. After hearing him play, the event’s host Billy Crystal remarked, “I’m thinking steroids. We should test him. Should we test him?”. sdfsdfsd
